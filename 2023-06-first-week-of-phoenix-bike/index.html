After trying Hunter's [Trek SuperCaliber 9.8 XT](https://www.trekbikes.com/ca/en_CA/bikes/mountain-bikes/cross-country-mountain-bikes/supercaliber/supercaliber-9-8-xt/p/35146) ultra-light XC ("cross country") racer bike for a week -- I was hooked. Once I saw this color scheme, and the 20% off sale for this 2022 bike -- I secretely ordered it. It's an absolutely amazing bike. It _wants_ you to push hard, to tear into a climb or bounce down a descent. She's named "Phoenix" after the X-Men superheroine, being a "supercaliber" bike from Trek in the sunburst/fireball colors of the Phoenix :) The (Shimano XT) brakes are *bananas* great. The stock gears are *perfection*. The (mechanical -- my preference still) shifting is supersmooth and the best I've ever had on a bike. I'm in love! At a hard-to-believe 24 pountds -- it's only 4 pounds more than my gravel bike. It's got 100mm of front fork travel, and 60mm in the rear (using this unique "inline" shock under the top tube). ![mountain bike in front of hedges](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-02.jpg) Quick parking lot shakedown ride before taking her home. ![bike being tuned](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-04.jpg) Matt Sharp of Lafayette "Sharp's Bikes" doing final adjustments for giddy new owner. ![tracey on new mountain bike in field](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-01.jpg) First offroad trail - absolutely loving it already! ![tracey and bike at Chabot Space & Science](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-00.jpg) At Chabot Space & Science Center ![mountain bike in front of Chabot Space & Science](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-03.jpg) Ultra-light mountain bike thanks NASA for light & strong technology :) ![mountain bike in redwoods](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-05.jpg) She loves to rip through trails in the Redwoods. ![mountain bike and flowers](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-11.jpg) Supercaliber meets the super bloom. ![mountain bike view from seat](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-07.jpg) Cockpit view ![tracey in group bike ride](../img/first-week-of-phoenix-bike-13.jpg) My first group ride ("Thirsty Thursday" with EBGB).
